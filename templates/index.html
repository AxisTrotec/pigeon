Purpose -


The purpose of this website is to initiate contact tracing. Contract tracing is the process in which restaurants can find potential people an infected person may have been in contact with.


Navigate -

ADD: Restaurant - Add restaurant information to the database.
ADD: Visit - Add information on visits to a restaurant to the database.
ADD: Infections - Add information on infected people to the database.
LIST: Restaurants - List all restaurant within the database.
LIST: Visits - List all the visit from a specific restaurant within the database.
LIST: Infections - List all the infected people within the database. As well as initiate a contact trace for a specific infectee.
